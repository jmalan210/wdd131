Pocatello, Idaho

Located in southeast Idaho, Pocatello was originally a railroad town. Its nickname is "gateway to the west". Pocatello is home to Idaho State University, and is surrounded by mountains on three sides. There are multiple outdoor activities available, including fishing, hiking, camping, boating, and more.
