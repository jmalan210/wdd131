About Me

Jennifer Malan grew up in Northern California, but now resides in beautiful Southeast Idaho with her family. She loves to read and travel. She earned a Bachelor's Degree in Health Science from Truman State University in Kirksville, MO in 2001, then was a full-time mom to her kids for many years. In recent years, Jennifer returned to school at Brigham Young University-Idaho to complete certificates in Social Media Marketing, Graphic Design and Computer Programming. Jennifer loves all things graphic design and would be delighted to help you with any project! See her portfolio page for examples of her work. Or contact her for more information!
